About

3 days of house, disco and techno in the woods.

2025 marks the 6th edition of Karrusel. Past editions include performances by Acid Pauli, Anetha, Amelie Lens, Ben Böhmer, Dixon, FJAAK, Folamour, Hunee, Job Jobse, KI/KI, Kölsch, Mall Grab, Marlon Hoffstadt, Nastia, Partiboi69, Peggy Gou, Richie Hawtin, Sama' Abdulhadi, Skin On Skin, The Blessed Madonna, Victor Ruiz, VTSS and more.

Karrusel takes place at a secluded green spot on Refshaleøen in central Copenhagen.
