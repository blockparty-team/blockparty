SUPPORT DISTORTION THROUGH GADEARMBÅND — GET EXCLUSIVE PERKS AND CREATE GOOD VIBES AT THE STREET PARTY


Because of YOU and your support throughout the years, Distortion can continue to celebrate Copenhagen! With the support-bracelets called ‘Gadearmbånd’ you help us celebrate Copenhagen streetlife and will get exclusive perks and free karma in return. With your help we can have even more events as long table dinners, pop-up celebrations, stages for new musical talents to perform as well as supporting your local shops and zealots - ultimately creating an improved street party for the years to come.

As a thank you for the supporting Distortion, for 100 kr. you get:

  • 15% on Tuborg in the bars
  • Exclusive access to Balcony Rides at Dronning Louises Bro
  • Exclusive access to Water Fountain
  • Half price on Silent Disco at Blågårds Plads
  • Half price on an Fresh Cut at the Hair Wheel of Doom at Elmegade 13
  • More perks will soon be announced...

You can buy the Gadearmbånd at the Distortion bars, and at our welcome portals at Dronning Louises Bro and Nørrebros Runddel.

We cannot wait to be together for a celebration of streetlife in Nørrebro and new music of Copenhagen at Distortion with YOU!
