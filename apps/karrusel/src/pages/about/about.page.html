About

3 days of house, disco and techno in the woods.

50+ artists across 4 stages including Anetha, Ben Böhmer, HorsegiirL, Job Jobse, Ki/Ki, Marlon Hoffstadt, Nene H, Sama’ Abdulhadi, Viken Arman, X CLUB.

Karrusel takes place at a secluded green spot on Refshaleøen in central Copenhagen.

2024 marks the 5th edition of Karrusel. Past editions include performances by Acid Pauli, Amelie Lens, Dixon, FJAAK, Folamour, Hunee, Job Jobse, Kölsch, Mall Grab, Nastia, Partiboi69, Peggy Gou, Richie Hawtin, Skin On Skin, The Blessed Madonna, Victor Ruiz, VTSS and more.
